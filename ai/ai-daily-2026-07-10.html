EU Council Approves 'Digital Omnibus' Amending AI Act to Postpone Deadlines

›
Published 2026-06-29

The Council of the European Union gave final approval to the "Digital Omnibus on AI," a legislative amendment that delays the compliance timeline for high-risk AI systems regulated under the EU AI Act. Under the revised schedule, the deadline for stand-alone high-risk systems (Annex III) has been pushed to December 2, 2027, while the deadline for product-embedded high-risk systems (Annex I) is extended to August 2, 2028.

Why it matters: The delay represents a major concession to European industries and developers struggling with the complexity of compliance, giving them breathing room but deferring strict regulatory enforcement of critical AI systems.
