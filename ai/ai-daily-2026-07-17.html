Mistral introduces Robostral Navigate for single-camera robot navigation

›

Company announcement · July 15. Mistral introduced Robostral Navigate, an 8B model that uses a single RGB camera and a natural-language instruction to move robots through complex environments. Mistral reports 76.6% success on unseen R2R-CE benchmarks and positions the system as a lower-sensor route into embodied AI.

Why it matters: If the result transfers beyond the company’s evaluation, robotics developers can reduce sensor and hardware requirements for visual navigation.

Thinking Machines releases Inkling, a trillion-parameter open multimodal model

›

Model release coverage · July 15. Thinking Machines released Inkling under Apache 2.0 with 975B total parameters, 41B active parameters, multimodal text-image-audio input, and a one-million-token context window. The release included integrations with Transformers, SGLang, vLLM, and llama.cpp.

Why it matters: Developers gain an adaptable frontier-scale multimodal foundation outside closed APIs, although its memory footprint limits direct deployment to substantial infrastructure.
