🏛️ Policy

EU orders Google to give rival AI assistants greater Android and Search access

›

Regulatory action · July 16. The European Union ordered Google to provide rival AI assistants with access to Android capabilities comparable to Gemini and to share selected Google Search data with competing search engines and AI services under the Digital Markets Act. Google has until January 2027 to begin sharing Search data and July 2027 to implement the Android changes.

Why it matters: The rulings could weaken Google’s distribution advantage in AI assistants and create new openings for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other rivals.
