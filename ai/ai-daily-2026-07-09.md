### LMCache/LMCache 🔥 exploding
A modular, high-performance Key-Value (KV) cache management layer for LLM inference. It enables the persistent storage, sharing, and reuse of KV caches (e.g. across vLLM and SGLang) to minimize redundant prefill computations and lower Time-to-First-Token.
Stats: ⭐ 10.2k · 🍴 1.5k · 📈 rapid · 🟢 active
Traction: Originated as academic research from University of Chicago; officially joined PyTorch Foundation in October 2025. Boasts contributions from NVIDIA, IBM, AMD, Microsoft, and CoreWeave.
References:

- https://pytorch.org/blog/lmcache-joining-pytorch/
- https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.09665
