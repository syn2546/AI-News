Google DeepMind open-weights DiffusionGemma — a diffusion LLM, not autoregressive

›
Published 2026-06-10

DiffusionGemma is an Apache-2.0, 26B-class MoE (~3.8B active) that generates text by denoising 256-token blocks in parallel instead of one token at a time, hitting 1,000+ tokens/sec on a single H100 — up to 4x faster than comparable autoregressive models. It quantizes to ~18GB VRAM so it runs locally; Google is candid that raw quality trails standard Gemma 4.

Why it matters: The first credible open-weights text-diffusion model puts a non-autoregressive architecture in every developer's hands, reopening a sidelined paradigm.
