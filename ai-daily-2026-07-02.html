Google DeepMind Introduces LEAP Agentic Framework for Formal Mathematics

›
Published 2026-06-01

Researchers from Google DeepMind and Google Cloud AI published LEAP (LLM-in-Lean Environment Agentic Prover), an agentic framework that decomposes complex theorems into Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) of lemmas, querying general-purpose LLMs and iteratively refining them based on compiler feedback. It successfully solved all 12 problems in the 2025 Putnam Competition and boosted the solve rate on Lean-IMO-Bench to 70%.

Why it matters: LEAP demonstrates that general-purpose foundation models, when paired with compiler-verified agentic loops, can match or exceed specialized mathematical provers without custom fine-tuning.
